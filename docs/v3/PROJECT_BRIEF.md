# Project Brief

## Objective

Build a trustworthy, fast, local-search-optimized production website that turns qualified homeowners into quote requests for Marlboro Manor Cleaning.

## Primary conversion

`Request an Estimate` → public Marble Quote Portal.

Secondary conversions:

- Call the business
- Email the business
- View service scope and pricing expectations
- Visit the verified Google Business Profile

## Audience

- Busy professionals and dual-income families
- Homeowners who prioritize reliability and care over lowest price
- Move-in/move-out customers with date-sensitive needs
- Residents within the company’s approved service radius

## Customer promise

Professional residential cleaning delivered with clear communication, meticulous care, and dependable systems.

## V3 release goals

- Replace deceptive/generated operational imagery with authentic or safely licensed visuals.
- Add factual trust signals and a visible local phone number.
- Create dedicated service and location pages.
- Integrate a real quote workflow through Marble.
- Establish local SEO, structured data, Search Console readiness, and analytics consent boundaries.
- Meet accessibility and performance requirements.

## Out of scope

- Customer accounts or payments
- Online appointment commitments
- Private Marble dashboard
- Employee/cleaner portal
- Blog publishing system unless specifically authorized
- Dozens of thin city pages
- Automated chat/voice agent
